Frequently Asked Questions

What industries do you specialize in for recruitment?

We specialize in IT and technology recruitment across all domains including software development, cybersecurity, data science, cloud computing, DevOps, and IT infrastructure. Our recruiters have technical backgrounds and industry-specific expertise.

How long does the recruitment process typically take?

The recruitment process typically takes 2-4 weeks from initial screening to job offer. This includes resume review, technical assessments, interviews, and reference checks. For executive positions, the process may take 4-6 weeks.

Do you charge candidates for your services?

No, our services are completely free for job seekers. We are paid by companies for successful placements. We offer career guidance, resume review, and interview preparation at no cost to candidates.

What is your success rate for placements?

We maintain a 92% success rate for placements, with an 85% retention rate after 12 months. Our thorough screening process and focus on cultural fit contribute to these high success rates.

Do you offer remote or contract positions?

Yes, we offer both remote and contract positions. Approximately 40% of our placements are for remote roles, and 25% are contract positions with potential for conversion to full-time employment.

How do I prepare for technical interviews?

We provide comprehensive interview preparation including technical question banks, mock interviews, and coding practice. Our career guidance service includes personalized preparation based on the specific role and company you're interviewing with.

What makes TalentBridge different from other agencies?

Our differentiators include: 1) Technical recruiters with industry experience, 2) AI-powered screening combined with human expertise, 3) Focus on cultural fit beyond technical skills, 4) Comprehensive career support for candidates, and 5) Long-term partnership approach with clients.
